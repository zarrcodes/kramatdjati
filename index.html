About Our Nation

The Republic of Kramat Jati stands as a modern beacon of classical Islamic civilization. Founded on the principles of justice, intellectual pursuit, and spiritual harmony, we bridge the wisdom of the ancients with the innovations of tomorrow.

Our vision is to cultivate a society where the ink of the scholar is revered. We operate under a philosophical framework that champions the preservation of historical manuscripts, the funding of universal education, and the promotion of ethical governance.

  • Dedication to classical knowledge and modern sciences
  • Preservation of Islamic architectural and literary heritage
  • Governance rooted in justice and ethical philosophy
  • Thriving community of scholars, artists, and thinkers

The Constitution

Article I

The Foundation of Knowledge

The Republic of Kramat Jati recognizes the pursuit of knowledge as a divine mandate and a fundamental human right. The State shall bear the responsibility of preserving scholarly heritage and ensuring unimpeded access to education for all its inhabitants.

Article II

Justice and Governance

All authority is a trust (Amanah). Governance shall be conducted through mutual consultation (Shura), bound by the ethical frameworks of Islamic jurisprudence, ensuring equity, protecting the marginalized, and upholding the dignity of every individual regardless of creed.

Article III

Sanctity of the Environment

The earth is a trust bestowed upon humanity. The Republic mandates the protection of nature, sustainable architecture, and the prohibition of excessive consumption that disrupts the ecological balance ordained by the Creator.
